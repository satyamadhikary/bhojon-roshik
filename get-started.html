VOJON ROSIK RESTAURANT Pvt. Ltd.

A Journey Through Bengali Flavors and Tradition

Welcome to Vojon Rosik Restaurant, where Bengal's love for good food comes alive on every plate. Rooted in authentic Bengali flavors and time-honored recipes, we bring you the comfort of home-style cooking blended with thoughtful creativity. From cherished traditional dishes to carefully crafted modern favorites, every meal at Vojon Rosik is prepared with passion, purity, and respect for Bengal's culinary legacy.

At Vojon Rosik, food isn't just served - it's celebrated. Come experience heartfelt hospitality, soulful flavors, and the true joy of Bengali cuisine.

Signature Experience

Fine-dine
Restaurant

Discover the refined flavors of Bengal in an elegant dine-in setting, supported by warm, attentive service. Enjoy signature plates such as Shorshe Ilish, Kosha Mangsho, and Chingri Machher Malai Curry, prepared with authentic recipes and premium ingredients.

Complete your meal with handcrafted Bengali desserts like Nolen Gurer Rosogolla and Makha Sandesh.

Celebrations & Events

Bring the flavors of Bengal to your celebrations with bespoke catering. From weddings and corporate events to family gatherings, our experienced team delivers exceptional service with menus tailored to every palate.

Choose from traditional Bengali favorites and modern fusion dishes to create a memorable dining experience.
